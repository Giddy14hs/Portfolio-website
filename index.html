About Me

I'm a software engineer with a passion for designing and developing web applications. I specialize in frontend development and have a strong understanding of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frontend frameworks like React, Node.js, and Tailwind CSS.

I work closely with UX/UI designers, backend developers, and other stakeholders to create responsive, efficient, and accessible web applications. My work history includes 1 year as a developer, with dozens of projects completed.

Projects

Job Board

The Job Board Website is a comprehensive platform designed to connect job seekers with potential employers. Job seekers can browse through job postings, track their applications, and manage their profiles, while employers can post job openings and manage applications.

Microfinance Website

The Microfinance Website is tailored for a small microfinance company. It offers a user-friendly interface for clients to access financial services such as loans, savings, and account management. The platform enables users to apply for loans, track their repayment schedules, and manage their accounts.

Food Menu Website

An online platform where guests can browse through the hotel's food offerings. The website showcases a dynamic and visually appealing menu that includes detailed descriptions of dishes, prices, and availability. Guests can easily navigate through different categories, such as breakfast, lunch, and dinner options.
